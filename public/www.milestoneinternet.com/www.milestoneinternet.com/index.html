GEO Intelligence Platform

Discover how AI engines see your brand and what to improve

Milestone's GEO solution and Visibility Dashboard provide critical insights for enhancing your brand's presence in AI-powered search. By monitoring topics, prompts, sentiment, and citations across online channels, it enables competitive benchmarking and content creation recommendations tailored to AI engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini. The dashboard also highlights missing content opportunities, ensuring your brand remains visible and competitive in the evolving digital landscape.

GEO-first CMS

Build websites that are visible in AI Search

Milestone's AI-native CMS integrates GEO and SEO to help your website rank higher in AI engines and traditional search. With built-in AI Content Studio, it streamlines content creation and shortens production cycles. Integrated AI Agents and personalization tools deliver engaging digital experiences that drive conversions and customer acquisition, resulting in 10-25% higher digital revenue.

AI Content Studio

Automate content workflows and create AI-optimized content at scale

Milestone's AI Content Studio automates the entire content workflow from ideation to publishing across multiple channels. It generates SEO and GEO-optimized content with keyword and prompt suggestions, re-optimization, and scalability, ensuring consistent brand voice across hundreds of locations. By streamlining the process, it reduces content creation time by 80-95%, saving both cost and effort.
Request Platform Demo for AI Content Studio Request Platform Demo - AI Content Studio

AI Agent Platform

Build trusted, high-quality agents for customer engagement and conversion

Milestone's AI Agent Platform leverages website, call center, ARI, and pricing data to provide trusted, high-quality customer interactions. The AI agent platform ensures precise semantic chunking to answer inquiries accurately. It includes guardrails to seamlessly hand off to live agents when necessary. AI agents enhance discovery, conversion, and post-sales by assisting with trip planning, bookings, payment integrations, reservation modifications, and upselling, boosting efficiency and customer satisfaction.
Request Platform Demo for AI Agent Platform Request Platform Demo - AI Agent Platform

Schema Manager

Deploy structured data that powers AI-first discovery

Milestone Schema Manager automates schema deployment, drift detection, and performance tracking at scale. With 800+ schema types and AI-driven automation, it ensures your content is optimized for AI search, increasing visibility in platforms like ChatGPT and Gemini. Stay ahead with accurate, scalable, and measurable schema management.
Request Platform Demo for Schema Manager Request Platform Demo - Schema Manager

Local Listings

Visibility across Google, Apple, Bing, and everywhere customers search

Milestone Local ensures consistent and accurate business information across search engines, directories, and maps by tracking and managing your URL, Name, Address, and Phone (UNAP) details. The platform removes duplicate listings to maintain data consistency and distributes your business information across channels like Google, Bing, Apple Maps, and more at scale. Whether managing a single location or hundreds, Milestone Local helps enhance visibility and drive local engagement with ease.
